Oceans

Large masses of water that cover around 70% of the land surface

The Pacific Ocean

Size: ~168 million km² (largest)
Average depth: ~4,000 m
Notable features: Mariana Trench (deepest point on Earth)
Borders: Asia, Oceania, Americas

Atlantic Ocean

Size: ~85 million km²
Average depth: ~3,646 m
Notable features: Mid-Atlantic Ridge (underwater mountain range)
Borders: Americas, Europe, Africa

Indian Ocean

Size: ~70 million km²
Average depth: ~3,741 m
Notable features: Warmest ocean, major trade routes
Borders: Africa, Asia, Australia

Southern Ocean

Size: ~21 million km²
Average depth: ~4,000–5,000 m
Notable features: Encircles Antarctica, strong currents (Antarctic Circumpolar Current)
Borders: Antarctica

Arctic Ocean

Size: ~21 million km²
Average depth: ~4,000–5,000 m
Notable features: Encircles Antarctica, strong currents (Antarctic Circumpolar Current)
Borders: Antarctica
